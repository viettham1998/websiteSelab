Welcome to Software Engineering Laboratory

SELab was established in 2002 as a pioneer laboratory on software engineering to develop useful systems and services
The research group focuses on the convergence of Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering, and Human-Computer Interaction to develop smart and secure interactive environments to better assist people in daily lives.
